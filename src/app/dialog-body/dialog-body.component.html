PRIVACY POLICY- NEWS ANYTIME

Our website provides users with the information about events happening around them.The closest offline equivalent to our website are newspapers and newspaper delivery services.As we know, information is power and we believe that we are empowering our users by providing them with this information.We follow a very strict privacy policy as protecting our users’ privacy and keeping their data safe is our topmost concern. User information is typically used to provide our users with better services and relevant news that they might be interested in knowing about. Below mentioned are the details about the information we collect, how we use it and share it for the growth of our business and the betterment of our users.
I. INFORMATION WE COLLECT
Users do not need to create accounts or provide their information just to read news. Hence, there is very little information that we absolutely need in order for our website to function. But, knowing the preferred categories of news for users would be helpful as we can provide them with more stories in these categories, all the different views people are sharing in these categories and so on. User contact information such as their email address would also be helpful to send them our newsletter. Additionally, this gives the user added capabilities such as to like, comment or share their views on the articles by our editors. Much like the offline equivalents, we would like to integrate ad services in our website to help us monetize our website.
II. HOW WE USE INFORMATION
We believe in providing our users with unbiased news updates. Hence, we do not sell user’s preferences and political views with the newsmakers. But we believe there is some data that will help us provide better articles to our users in relevant categories and domains they are interested in. For instance, we collect preferred categories from users while registration so that we can provide them an easier way to navigate to those categories.We also collect user contact information such as their email address which helps us in sending them our newsletter.
III. HOW WE SHARE INFORMATION
Unfortunately, using external ad services in our website would inevitably expose our users to a variety of tracking mechanisms, but this is unavoidable, since we need the revenue generated by these ads to monetize our website. We do not sell, trade, rent or otherwise share for marketing purposes our users’ Personal Information such as their email address, date of birth, contact information, etc., which they submit to us through the registration process at the site, with third parties without their consent.We also use our users’ Non-Personal Information such as anonymous usage data, general demographic information we may collect, referring/exit pages and URLs, in order to track trends and analyze use patterns on our site. This information would be shared with the advertisers and other third parties appearing on our site.
